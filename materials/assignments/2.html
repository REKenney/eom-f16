Assignment #2

Variability: Designing from Inspiration, a Series

  • Course Home

Choose an artist or designer you are inspired by. Gather 3-5 digital images of their work you find most inspiring. Make a list of 3-5 reasons you find the chosen images inspiring. Do you like the colors, textures, scale, mood they use?

Pick your favorite image. Create a Processing sketch inspired by 1 or 2 aspects of this image. Hint: you are not trying to copy or re-create the inspiration image, instead you are using attributes or aspects of the image to use as inspiration.

Create 10 variations of your sketch, just but changing variables, constants, and random values.

Deliverables

Sync all code deliverables to your github student repository, and all videos to YouTube by 6:30pm on the due date.

  1. Your Processing Sketch source-code inspired by your artist or designer.
  2. Create 10 variations of your sketch by varying the values of variables, or taking screenshots over time (if your sketch changes over time). Does your series tell a story? Sync all 10 to GitHub.
