Interactive Poster

Design an “interactive poster” that could be installed in the MassArt Design & Media Center Lobby. Assume your piece will be projected onto a wall, with a podium containing a mouse and keyboard. In traditional poster design, the designer seeks to quickly convey a message with a static visual image. Your assignment is to create a new kind of poster, an “interactive poster.”

Using Processing and any of the skills you have learned create a sketch that will be displayed on the large monitor. You may use shapes, images, video, sound, etc… Like a poster your imagery and sound should entice and engage the user. Your “poster” must convey a message you find important. Topic ideas include but are not limited to: race/gender/economic equality, housing & food access, global warming, women's reproductive rights, nuclear energy, war, gun violence, hunger, communication, virtual reality, etc…

Requirements: your sketch must take advantage of the mouse and keyboard and respond to input from a user. You may require as little or as much interaction from the user as you desire.

Feel free to pick a different location (than MassArt) if you have one in mind.

Deliverables

Submit all deliverables to Google Drive by 5pm on due date.

  1. Upload your final sketch to Google Drive. You may upload multiple variations if you desire.
  2. If you have really large videos start uploading before the deadline to ensure you hand in your assignment on-time.
